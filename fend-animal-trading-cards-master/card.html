A Cat.

Cats don't always chase mice to kill them. Many cats chase simply to hone their hunting skills. They also hone their skills by playing with siblings and toys.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Avergae Length: 18 in.
  • Average Lifespan: 4 - 5 years
  • Habitat: Not their bed/wherever you sleep

They are lazy. They reflect how humans would love to be treated. No responsibilities, fundamentally ungrateful, and full of conditional love. We love them because they seem to not need us.
